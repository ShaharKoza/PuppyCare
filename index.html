Features

Everything your dog needs.
In one app.

From live sensor tiles to an AI food safety assistant — PuppyCare is a complete care platform, not just a monitoring tool.

📡

Live Sensor Dashboard

Temperature, humidity, motion, bark count, light state, and kennel presence timer — updating live from your Raspberry Pi.

🚨

Smart Alerts

Automatic warnings for extreme temperatures, sustained barking, and motion events. Full history with analytics and bark frequency charts.

📷

On-Demand Camera

Tap to request a live snapshot from the kennel. The Pi captures and uploads it to Firebase within seconds.

🍖

Feeding & Routine

Daily meal schedule with gram tracking, calorie calculation, and a next-event badge for every routine item.

🤖

AI Food Assistant

Type any food name and get an instant safety rating — safe, caution, or dangerous — with an explanation tailored for dogs.

🔔

Push Notifications

Firebase Cloud Functions send FCM push alerts to your phone for warning, stress, and emergency levels — even in the background.

How It Works

Hardware meets software.

Four layers working together to give you a complete picture of your dog's environment in real time.

Step 01

Sensors Read

Raspberry Pi reads DHT22, PIR, sound, and light sensors every few seconds and writes JSON to Firebase paths.

Step 02

Firebase Syncs

Firebase Realtime Database propagates the update instantly to all connected listeners via WebSocket.

Step 03

App Updates

SwiftUI views re-render automatically as @Published sensor state changes on the main actor.

Step 04

You're Notified

Cloud Function triggers on critical alerts and sends an FCM push notification to your device within seconds.
